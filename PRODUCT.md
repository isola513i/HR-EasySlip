# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Thai HR Managers (50–300 employees), Operations Directors at multi-branch SMEs, SME owners who handle HR themselves. Secondary: CFOs and COOs who need payroll export. Context: working at a desk during business hours, comparing HR systems or onboarding a new tool. Familiar with Excel-based workflows; skeptical of complex enterprise software.

## Product Purpose

EasySlip HR is a multi-tenant SaaS HR platform built specifically for Thai organizations. It centralizes attendance (GPS clock-in), leave management, OT, expenses, and payroll export (Empeo integration) in one system — replacing fragmented Excel workflows. Each company gets an isolated subdomain and database. Free-trial signup, no credit card required.

## Brand Personality

Operationally mature, direct, Thai-first. Three words: reliable, purposeful, legible. Emotional goal: the confidence of a senior HR manager who knows the system will not embarrass them in an audit or leave a payroll error unexplained.

## Anti-references

- Fake testimonials, fabricated logos, placeholder avatars — forbidden (no customers yet)
- Unsupported quantitative claims ("60% less work", "saves 10 hours") — forbidden
- Strong legal compliance claims without implementation ("รองรับ พ.ร.บ. 2568 ครบ") — forbidden; use operational wording instead
- Childish, playful, or startup-casual tone — wrong register
- Dark purple drench or neon fintech — too aggressive for HR buyers
- SaaS-cream (off-white + teal) — category reflex, avoid
- Generic card grids with icon + heading + body repeated across all sections

## Design Principles

1. **Show, don't promise.** Real product screenshots over marketing copy. If the product looks good, let it speak.
2. **Operational maturity reads as restraint.** Color is used as a signal, not decoration. Every gradient earns its place.
3. **Thai context first.** Thai labor law, familiar approval flows, and PDPA-aligned language come before international SaaS patterns.
4. **Trust through specificity.** Concrete product facts (isolated DB, Empeo integration, audit log) outperform generic claims.
5. **No friction before value.** Trial signup in under 2 minutes, no credit card, workspace live in 24 hours.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Thai + English bilingual (i18n required on all user-facing strings). Focus-visible rings on all interactive elements. Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`. Touch targets ≥ 44px for PWA employee app.
